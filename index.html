The Most Air-Tight Encryption Available

Mailum is the only email service encrypting all 4 elements of an email and its metadata. While other services only encrypt the email body, we encrypt body, subject, sender, recipient, and timestamp to protect your online privacy from unauthorized access.

Peace of mind without relying on trust!

Our top priority while designing and building Mailum was to create an email service that is not based on reliance.
We came up with a solution named Local Code.
We provide users with the ability to host the Mailum website on their computers locally.
This allows users to have an absolute certainty that scripts loaded by their browsers have neither been maliciously modified nor been altered.
This is optional, however, available to anyone who feels a little more concerned and paranoid.
