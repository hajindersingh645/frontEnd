Mailum Technologies

Our top priority while building Mailum was to create an email service that is not based on reliance. We came up with a solution named "Local Code".

We provide users with the ability to host the Mailum website on their computers locally. This allows users to have an absolute certainlty that scripts loaded by their browsers have neither been maliciously modified nor been altered.
